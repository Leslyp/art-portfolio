Background

I was born in El Salvador, and moved to Charlotte, North Carolina when I was around three years old. I've spent most of my life here in Charlotte, but hope to travel in the future to destinations like Italy, France, and Canada. Some of my hobbies include, writing poetry and swimming

Art

Growing up, art has always been an interest of mine. All throughout school, I continued to create art and find different ways to incorporate it into my life. Some of the forms of art I enjoy using are painting, sketching, and watercolors. What I love about art is the constant stream of endless possibilities you can create. Not only do I admire the unique artworks I can create, but in some ways, I enjoy more experiencing and learning from the creations of others.

Current Occupation

Aside from doing art, I currently work at a company named Red Ventures as a Front-end Developer. Coding is a newly found passion of mine, and I'm excited to see where it takes me. It's actually what allowed me to learn how to create this website that your viewing right now.
